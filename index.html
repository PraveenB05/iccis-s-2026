About Sona College of Technology

Sona College of Technology has achieved global identity through its involvement in research and innovation. The institution is accredited with NAAC A++ and recognized for excellence in education, research, and industry collaboration. The college has world-class infrastructure, highly qualified faculty, and strong industry interaction.


About the Department of CSE

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering is committed to excellence in education, research, and innovation, with a strong emphasis on producing technically competent and socially responsible professionals. The department offers well-structured undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to meet global industry standards and emerging technological trends. The department also actively promotes student development through technical clubs, workshops, hackathons, seminars, and guest lectures by industry experts and academicians. By nurturing ethical values, leadership qualities, and lifelong learning skills, the Department of Computer Science and Engineering strives to prepare graduates who can excel in academia, research, entrepreneurship, and the global IT industry.


About ICCISS 2026


ICCISS 2026 provides an international platform for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to exchange ideas and discuss recent advances in computational intelligence, security, and intelligent systems. The conference includes keynote lectures, invited talks, and peer-reviewed paper presentations.
